Comments

Blot does not have a built-in commenting system. However, you can install any of the following comment services with a few clicks on the Services page of your site's dashboard.

  • Disqus
  • Bluesky comments

Controlling comments on individual entries

When enabled, comments are shown on posts and are not shown on pages. You can change this for individual posts and pages by setting the Comments to Yes or No in the metadata.

Switching commenting systems

You can replace the commenting system on your site with a few clicks on the Services page of your site's dashboard.
