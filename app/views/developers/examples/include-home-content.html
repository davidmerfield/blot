Include home page content above recent entries

This example shows how to reuse the content from your Home.md page at the top of your template while still listing your recent entries underneath.

Add a new view (or update your existing home view) with the recommended metadata and the partial include for your page content followed by your recent entries.

Page: Yes
Menu: No
---

Your blog

  {{> /Pages/Home.md}}

  {{#recentEntries}}
  

    
{{title}}

    {{{body}}}
  

  {{/recentEntries}}

The {{> /Pages/Home.md}} line pulls the contents of your Home.md file into the template, ensuring it appears before the loop of recentEntries.
