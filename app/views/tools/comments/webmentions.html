Webmentions

Webmentions let you collect replies, likes, and reposts from other sites. Blot's Webmentions plugin uses webmention.io to fetch those conversations and show them beneath your posts.

Set up webmention.io

  1. Visit webmention.io and sign in with one of the supported providers.

  2. Add your site's domain (for example, example.com) to your webmention.io account. Follow the prompts to verify ownership of the domain — webmention.io will ask you to add a short verification token to your site before continuing.

  3. In the webmention.io dashboard, ensure that Receive Webmentions (incoming webmentions) is enabled for your domain. This allows the service to accept and store mentions on your behalf.

  4. Copy the Webmention and Pingback endpoint URLs that webmention.io shows for your domain. Add them to your site's template inside the <head> element, either by editing your custom template or by using the Custom > Head setting: 

    <link rel="webmention" href="https://webmention.io/yourdomain/webmention">
<link rel="pingback" href="https://webmention.io/yourdomain/xmlrpc">

  5. Enable the Webmentions service on Blot's Services page. Once enabled, Blot will load the widget on posts and display mentions automatically when webmention.io reports them for each URL.

New mentions can take a few minutes to appear after they are received by webmention.io. The widget only appears on your site when there are mentions to show, so you can enable it ahead of time without affecting existing posts.
