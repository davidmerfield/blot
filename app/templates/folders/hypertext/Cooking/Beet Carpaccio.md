# Beet Carpaccio

*A minimalist plate, beautiful and earthy.*

**Ingredients**
- 3 medium beets, roasted
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- Salt, pepper
- Shaved Parmesan
- Arugula

**Method**
1. Slice beets thinly. Arrange on plate.
2. Drizzle oil and vinegar. Season.
3. Top with arugula and Parmesan.
