# Grilled Sardines with Lemon and Oregano

A simple Greek summer dish, often eaten by the sea.

**Ingredients**
- 8 fresh sardines, cleaned
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 lemon
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt, pepper

**Method**
1. Rinse and pat dry the sardines. Brush with oil, season with salt and oregano.
2. Grill for 2â€“3 minutes per side until skin blisters and flesh just flakes.
3. Squeeze lemon over the fish before serving.
4. *Footnote: Charcoal grilling adds essential flavour; a hot pan is the next best thing.*
