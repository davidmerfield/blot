# Lentil and Chard Stew

Common in mountain villages from Liguria to Aragón. Nutritious, earthy, and inexpensive.

**Ingredients**
- 250 g brown lentils
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 carrot, diced
- 1 tomato, grated
- 1 bunch Swiss chard, roughly chopped
- Olive oil
- 1.2 L water or vegetable stock
- Salt, pepper

**Method**
1. In a heavy pot, sauté onion, garlic, and carrot in olive oil until golden.
2. Add lentils and grated tomato. Stir for one minute.
3. Add stock and simmer uncovered until lentils are tender (30–35 minutes).
4. Add chard and cook 5 minutes more. Season to taste.
5. Serve with olive oil drizzled over the top.
