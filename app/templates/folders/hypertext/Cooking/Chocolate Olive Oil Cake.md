# Chocolate Olive Oil Cake

*Moist, dark, and perfectly simple.*

**Ingredients**
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup olive oil
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup coffee or hot water
- 1 tsp baking soda
- Pinch salt

**Method**
1. Whisk dry ingredients. Add wet. Stir until smooth.
2. Pour into greased tin and bake at 175°C (350°F) for 35 minutes.
3. Cool completely before slicing.
