# Miso Black Cod

*The Nobu classic, adapted for the patient home cook.*

**Ingredients**
- 2 black cod fillets
- 3 tbsp white miso paste
- 2 tbsp sake
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 1 tbsp sugar

**Method**
1. Mix miso, sake, mirin, and sugar into a smooth paste.
2. Marinate fish 24 hours in refrigerator.
3. Broil or bake at 200°C (400°F) for ~10 minutes until caramelized.
4. Serve with pickled ginger and steamed rice.
