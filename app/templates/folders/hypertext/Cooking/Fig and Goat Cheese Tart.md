# Fig and Goat Cheese Tart

*Sweet, savory, and faintly autumnal.*

**Ingredients**
- 1 sheet puff pastry
- 4 figs, sliced
- 100g goat cheese
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 sprig rosemary, chopped

**Method**
1. Lay pastry on baking sheet, score 1cm border.
2. Spread goat cheese, arrange figs, drizzle honey, sprinkle rosemary.
3. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 18–20 minutes.
