Whole moong beans
- 3 to 1 water to beans
- 1m high pressure
- 5m pressure release
- salt, pepper, whole cumin

Chickpeas:
- soak overnight
- 3 to 1 water to beans
- 13m high pressure -- they come out soft -- 11m
- 10m pressure release
- salt, pepper, whole cumin

Black beans
- no soak
- 3/1 water/beans
- 24m high pressure
- salt, pepper, whole cumin

Pinto beans
- no soak
- 3/1 water/beans
- 50m high pressure
- salt, pepper, whole cumin

Green lentils 
- no soak
- 1.75 water to 1 lentils
- 8m high pressure 
- salt pepper whole cumin 

Brown rice
- 1.25 water to 1 rice 
- 20m high pressure 20m release
