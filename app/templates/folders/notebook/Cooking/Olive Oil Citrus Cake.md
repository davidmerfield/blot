# Olive Oil Citrus Cake

Light, aromatic, and not too sweet. Keeps well for several days.

**Ingredients**
- 200 g flour
- 150 g sugar
- 3 eggs
- 120 ml olive oil
- Zest of 1 orange and 1 lemon
- 100 ml milk
- 1 tsp baking powder
- Pinch salt

**Method**
1. Whisk eggs and sugar until pale. Stir in olive oil, milk, and zest.
2. Fold in flour, baking powder, and salt until smooth.
3. Pour into a lined tin and bake at 180°C for 35–40 minutes.
4. Cool completely before slicing. Serve plain or with yoghurt.
