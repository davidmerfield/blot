# Risotto al Limone

A bright, citrus variation of northern Italian *risotto bianco*.

**Ingredients**
- 1 shallot, finely chopped
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 300 g Arborio rice
- 120 ml dry white wine
- 800 ml hot vegetable stock
- Zest and juice of 1 unwaxed lemon
- 40 g Parmesan, grated
- Salt

**Method**
1. Saut√© the shallot in butter and oil until translucent. Add rice; stir until coated and slightly translucent.
2. Pour in wine, stir until absorbed.
3. Add stock one ladle at a time, stirring constantly. After about 16 minutes, the rice should be creamy but still firm.
4. Stir in lemon zest and juice, then Parmesan. Let rest, covered, 2 minutes before serving.
